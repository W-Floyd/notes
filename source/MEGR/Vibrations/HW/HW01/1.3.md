# Solution

## Goal

Find spring stiffness $k$

## Given

$$
\begin{aligned}
    !ADefGiven(x)(x)(0.01)(\mathrm{~m})\\
    !ADefGiven(m)(m)(15)(\mathrm{~kg})\\
\end{aligned}
$$

## Assumptions

At sea level, so gravity is:

$$
    !DefGiven(g)(g)(9.81)(!mpsq)
$$

## Working

We know that:

$$
f_{k}=k x
$$

and knowing that:

$$
f=ma
$$

we can say that:

$$
ma=kx
$$

which becomes:

$$
k = \frac{mg}{x}
$$

Plugging values in:

$$
\begin{aligned}
    k &= \frac{!m\cdot!g}{!x}\\
    !Answer(k)(=)(!math{!m*!g/!x}!npm)
\end{aligned}
$$
