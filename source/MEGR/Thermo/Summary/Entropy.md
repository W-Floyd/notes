## Ideal gas

$$
\begin{array}{l}
s\left(T_{2}, v_{2}\right)-s\left(T_{1}, v_{1}\right)=\int_{T_{1}}^{T_{2}} c_{v}(T) \frac{d T}{T}+R \ln \frac{v_{2}}{v_{1}} \\
s\left(T_{2}, p_{2}\right)-s\left(T_{1}, p_{1}\right)=\int_{T_{1}}^{T_{2}} c_{p}(T) \frac{d T}{T}-R \ln \frac{p_{2}}{p_{1}}
\end{array}
$$

$$
