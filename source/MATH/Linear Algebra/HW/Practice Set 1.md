## 7

$$
2 x-4 y=0
$$

### Solution

$$
\begin{aligned}
2x-4y&=0\\
2x&=4y\\
x&=2y\\\\
\text{let} y&=t\\
\therefore !Aboxed{x}{=}{2t}\\
!Aboxed{y}{=}{t}
\end{aligned}
$$
