## Unit Vector

Unit vector is a vector with magnitude 1.

$\vec{u}$ !st $||\vec{u}||=1$

![](!imgdir/screenshot_toTCFo8rND0KeQIZ.png)

$u_1 = \cos{\theta} \qquad u_2 = \sin{\theta}$

Thus:

$||\vec{u}||=\sqrt{\cos^2{\theta} + \sin^2{\theta}}=1$

Pythagorean Identity
